Utkarsh

I am a undergraduate research student at the Center for Computational Natural Sciences and Bioinformatics (CCNSB), IIIT Hyderabad, advised by Professor Harjinder Singh and Subhadip Mitra. My research interest lie in Dynamical Systems and Networks, Machine Learning, Quantum Computing and in general application of Computer Science in Interdisciplinary fields.

I am currently working on the simulating Non-linear Dynamical systems on a Quantum Computer with Prof. Subhadip Mitra and on Mathematical basis for consciousness with Prof. Harjinder Singh. I also previously worked with Prof. Geoffrey Hutchison on on the project 'Active Learning based Bayesian Geometry Optimizer' as a part of Google Summer of Code 2023. The main goal of the project deals was generating the lowest energy conformer for a given molecule using Bayesian optimization locally in internal coordinates (i.e. bond lengths, bond angles, dihedral angle etc) and finding good initial estimates for the internal co-ordinates given a molecule. You can read more about the project here and my project blog here.

Besides academics, I am also a trained sketch Artist by training. I like writing poetries, playing piano/guitar in my free time. I work as a volunteer for teaching class 7th on weekends. If someone is also interested in volunteering, they can check out Make a Difference (MAD) NGO.
